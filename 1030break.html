Quick and Easy Breakfast

Most breakfasts are meant to be prepared rather quickly in order to get a start on the day, so most familiar breakfasts fall into the 10 to 30 minute category.

Generated Recipes!!

French Toast
French toast is a classic breakfast item, and it's so simple that most kid's cookbooks have recipes for it.
Homemade Biscuits
Biscuits are rarely served by themselves, but making these biscuits with some of the gravy recipes can be delicious and hearty in themselves. Also, most gravies require grease which comes from cooking breakfast meat.
Omelettes
Omelettes are a classic breakfast made mostly with eggs. They can be as simple as eggs with a little cheese, to whole vegetable and meat medleys cooked into them.
