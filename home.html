Welcome, I am FoodBert

Hi, my name is FoodBert and I am the cooking robot. That is, I don't actually cook, I can only help you select the recipe that you want to cook. It's no magic but I'm pretty amazing if I do say so myself (and I do). All you have to do is tell me what you're in the mood for, and I'll give you a couple options to perfectly suit your needs. Of course you still have to buy the ingredients and do the cooking, but let's face it, I've taken care of the hard part. So start off by letting me know below what you are looking to cook.
