Russian Sides

Russian food tends to be the ultimate comfort food. The heavy use of potatoes, cabbage, sour cream, and fatty meats makes for a perfect relaxing evening at home. My filter may be malfunctioning slightly, so there is a chance a couple Polish dishes and even a German one may slip in here.

Blinchiki
Blinchiki are served as small savory crepes. They can be customized with many different fillings, including vegetarian ones. The classic blinchiki are made with beef, onions and seasoning.
Olivie
Olivie is essentially a potato salad made with a lot of meat, typically sausage. You could make vegetarian olivie, but it would just be potato salad.
Solyanka
If Borscht is a Russian style soup, Solyanka is an olive style Borscht. It is made with olives, oil and chunks of meat more often than not.
