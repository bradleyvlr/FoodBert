Cold Alcoholic Beverages

When you are feeling a bit too creative to just have a beer or wine, you can try serving one of these beverages. They go particularly well with a nice evening on the porch.

Sangria Roja
The Red Sangria is a traditional Spanish drink. It is made from Red wine and fresh fruit, often with other ingredients to increase sweetness or Alcohol content. This pairs well either with a good meal or with a good party.
Sangria Blanca
The White Sangria is a little bit lighter than its red counterpart, and is often a bit sweeter. There are so many variations on making sangria, that this recipe will provide the basic essence of making it with a few options.
Classic Margarita
The margarita is a classic and can be served chilled or frozen (like a slushy). This recipe is for the base and will offer a few variations for customizeable margaritas.
